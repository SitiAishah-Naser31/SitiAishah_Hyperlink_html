The best time of the year is upon us: summertime. It's when get outdoors and connect with nature more whether it's through a day at the beach or a lovely hike. It's when we skip our nightly Netflix binges and opt for dinner and drinks with friends. It's when the weight of the world starts to feel a little lighter, even if only for a minute.

To honor the season, we rounded up some funny, inspiration and happy quotes all about summer and the goodness it can bring. So if you need a great caption for your Instagram beach photo or just need the right words to capture your summer vibes, these sayings will do the trick.

And remember, those three months ago by fast, especially since for most of us long summer vacations are a thing of the past, so make sure you enjoy every minute you have by planning some warm-weather activities, reading a great beach book and hosting your fair share of barbeques and picnics.
